( Chain A Residue number 1 Residue name LEU Atom name HA)

     'ambiguous NOE distance constraints'

be represented by a standard single pseudoatom.

( Chain A        Residue number 1 Residue name LEU Atom name HA)

length as well as the directionality of the bond.

    'chemical shift constraints'                
;
Chemical shift of e.g. a proton or carbon nucleus implemented as a constraint.
;
     'symmetry constraints'                      
;
Restraining (or constraining) symmetry between multiple copies of (part of) 
similar structures.
