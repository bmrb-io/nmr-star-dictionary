     _Item_enumeration.Description

    'Extremely slow exchange'      
;

;
    'Slow exchange'                
;

;
    'Intermediate exchange'        
;
May not observe signals from the component or signals may be quite broad.
;
    'Fast exchange'

;
On the NMR time scale$ a single averaged signal will be observed for the components in the bound and free state.
;
    'No interaction'
;
