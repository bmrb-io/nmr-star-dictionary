save__Assembly_interaction.Mol_interaction_type

  loop_
     _item_enumeration_value
     _item_enumeration_description

    'Extremely slow exchange'      
;
Components are very tightly bound
;
    'Slow exchange'                
;
Would expect to observe separate resonances for the bound and free components
of the system.
;
    'Intermediate exchange'        
;
May not observe signals from the component or signals may be quite broad.
;
    'Fast exchange'

;
On the NMR time scale$ a single averaged signal will be observed for the
components in the bound and free state.
;
    'No interaction'
;
The components exist in the sample but do not interact (i.e.$ a molecule that
is exchanging between a native and an unfolded conformation).
;
